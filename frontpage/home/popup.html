Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup sliced onions
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon fajita seasoning
  • 4 small flour tortillas
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until browned, about 5-6 minutes.
  3. Add the sliced bell peppers and onions to the skillet and cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the garlic, fajita seasoning, salt, and pepper to the skillet and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
  6. Assemble the fajitas by slicing the cooked chicken and vegetables, and serving with the warm tortillas.
